Instructions

First you will choose a fighter from the Star Wars Universe by clicking on their image.

Second you will choose a starting opponent from the remaining collection of characters by clicking on them.

You will then clash with your opponent until one of you is left alive. Clash with your opponent by clicking on the "Clash!" button.

Each time you clash with an opponent your power will increase! But be warned, your opponent hits you back each time.

If you reduce your opponent's hp to 0, they will be defeated and you can then select a new opponent.

Keep going until all of your opponents (or you) lie defeated!

Try to win with every character!
